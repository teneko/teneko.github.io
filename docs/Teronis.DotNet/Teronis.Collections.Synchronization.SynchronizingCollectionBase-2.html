    Class SynchronizingCollectionBase<SuperItemType, SubItemType>

    A synchronizing collection.
    Bindable collections are SubItems and SuperItems.
    Remember: All childs are parents (because child extends parent), so all childs are a subset of parent / parent is a superset of child.
